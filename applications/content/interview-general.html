Guidelines
  1. Find the application. (If he/she has not made one yet, ask them to).
  2. Make sure the application has been accepted before you proceed.
  3. Create a TED. (Link to Instructions)
  4. Change the Application’s Prefix to Trial (Link to Instructions)
  5. Comment on the application saying that the trial has started on {start-trial-date-long} and lasts until {end-trial-date-long} and that you've spoken to the person.
  6. Add the link to the TED to the trialist’s description on Teamspeak (Right click on client - Change description) and give them Trialist permissions.
  7. Explain everything (How a TED works, what it is, forum/ts/game activity, etc)
  8. Explain any further relevant information for the specific game (links, rules, groups, etc)
  9. Change the forum usergroup from Guest to Trialist. (If you do not know how to do this, you can find instructions here)
  10. Remind and encourage the new Trialist to be active on both TS and the Forums. (People can fail trials because of this now)
